At a glance

Bonobo LottoSuite enables you to rapidly build and deploy your Lottery by offering you a complete web presence, a library of customizable lottery games, and the means to sell tickets online and retail.

Administration

You and your staff can access your back-office from any device with a  browser.  For the best experience please use Google Chrome.

Staff can be granted access to each area of your back-office based on their Roles.  From setting up the site and modifying system configurations, to handling customer service emails or managing Games, Players and Sales agents. 

 

Online Sales

Your players can register and play from your branded web site, from any device. 

You can easily customize the look and feel of your site by updating the Logo, colors and fonts.

 

 

 

Direct Debit Subscription Lottery (UK)

This solution provides you with everything you need to start your own Monthly Subscription Lottery.

In the UK, this bundle includes Direct Debit processing with RapiData.

 

Retail Sales

Your sales agents can use any smart device to  sell tickets, pay prizes, verify players and sell Direct Debit Subscriptions (UK) in person or over the telephone.

Game Library

You can create your own custom games or choose from any of our pre-defined games.
