At a glance

Bonobo LottoSuite enables you to rapidly build and deploy your Online and Retail Lottery. 

Use this Wiki to learn more about how to use the system and its features. 

Access the live demo of the Bonobo Lottery platform and explore for yourself.

Please contact us if you have any question and suggestions or would like to report an issue.

Administration

You and your staff can access your back-office from any device with a  browser.  For the best experience please use Google Chrome.

Staff can be granted access to each area of your back-office based on their Roles.  

You can manage your Lottery Games, Staff, Players and Sales agents  from one place.

 

Online Sales

Your players can register and play from your branded web site, from any device. 

You can easily customize the look and feel of your site by updating the Logo, colors and fonts.

 

 

 

Direct Debit Subscription Lottery (UK)

Available for licensed charities and non-profits in the UK, the Monthly Subscription Lottery solution includes your own custom Lottery Game, and comes integrated with Direct Debit payment processing. 

You can sell your lottery subscriptions online as well through canvassers and retail agents.

 

Retail Sales

Your sales agents can use any smart device to  sell tickets, pay prizes, verify players and sell Direct Debit Subscriptions (UK) in person or over the telephone.

Game Library

You can create your own custom games or choose from any of our pre-defined games.
